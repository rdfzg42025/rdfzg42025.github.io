Physics of Microplastics

Introduction

This page introduces two numerical simulations designed to model the behavior of microplastics in different water bodies: a river and a circular lake. We used advection-dispersion equation (ADE) to stimulate the microplastic behavior in water bodies. ADE provides a model for the movement of particles due to the flow of water and the spreading of particles due to turbulence and molecular dispersion.

Advection-Dispersion Equation

The advection-dispersion equation (ADE) used in our simulations models the movement and spreading of microplastics in water bodies. It combines the effects of water flow and turbulence-induced dispersion.
